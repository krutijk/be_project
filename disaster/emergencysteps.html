Emergency Steps


  • Be alert. If a flash flood warning is issued for your area: Climb to safety immediately.
  • Don’t drive unless you have to. If you must drive, travel with care.
  • NEVER drive through flooded roadways. STOP! Turn Around Don’t Drown.
  • Get to high ground – Climb to safety!
  • Evacuate immediately, if you think you are at risk or are advised to do so!
  • Never try to walk or swim through flowing water.
  • Shut off the electricity at the circuit breakers.
  • If someone falls in or is trapped in flood water: Do not go after the victim!Use a floatation device.
  • If possible throw the victim something to help them float, such as a spare tire, large ball, or foam ice chest.
  • Call and inform your dear ones who may be near you.
  • Issue alert on the website.
